Hexapawn

A test of strategy with Hans, the Divination Wizard

Rules

  • Pawns move forward one square (if empty).
  • Pawns capture diagonally forward.
  • Win by: reaching the opposite side, capturing all opponent pawns, or leaving your opponent with no legal moves.
  • You play as white (bottom row).
Your turn! Click on a pawn and then its destination.
